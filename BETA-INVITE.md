## 2. What to tell testers to try (optional quick-start)

Paste this if a tester wants direction:

> **3 things to try in HaulYard:**
> 1. **Post a request** (Buy tab → New Request) — say what material you need and where.
> 2. **Post a listing** (Sell tab → New Listing) — list material you have available.
> 3. **Browse the map** (Buy → Map View) — see what's nearby.
>
> Everyone can use all three sides (buy, sell, haul) — switch with the bottom tabs.
> Hit **"Send feedback"** anytime to tell me what you think.

## 3. Where feedback goes

Every "Send feedback" submission is **emailed straight to alex@eclsite.com** (and stored
in the app). You'll see them land in your inbox as testers use it.

## Good to know (current beta limits — not blockers)

- **Free-tier-style wake-up:** first hit after idle is slow (~30–60s), then fast. (Already
  on the always-on paid plan, so this is minimal.)
- **Email deliverability:** your domain is authenticated, so emails should hit the inbox.
  If an early tester says a verification/reset email went to spam, have them mark "Not spam."
- **Data:** persists across app updates (permanent storage is on).
- **Billing:** hidden — it's free for everyone during the beta.

## When you're ready to grow beyond a small group
- **Invite-only gating** (require a code to sign up) — keeps the beta controlled.
- **Error monitoring** (e.g. Sentry) — see crashes testers hit.
- **Postgres** — swap the JSON store for a database built for many concurrent users.

These can wait until the closed beta gives you signal. For now — **go invite people.** 🎉
